EDI

EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) in Bring and Posten started in the 90’s. EDI Guidelines based on different versions of EDIFACT and API have been published since then, Iftmin s93a, d97a and API. Today’s version is based on the D04A standards.

Bring and Posten still support some early versions, however they will not be updated with changes. We strongly suggest all our clients to use D04A or XML.

Transport Instruction for IFTMIN 04

This document is our guideline for freight-messages based on IFTMIN D04A EDIFACT. The overview and detailed guideline include all Bring services, additional services and surcharges with codes for setting up correct EDI-messages.
